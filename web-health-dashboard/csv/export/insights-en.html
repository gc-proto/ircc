Traffic decreased slightly, which is typical for this time of year.

Search engines continue to drive most traffic to IRCC content.

Of the traffic coming from social networks, over half of traffic is referred from Facebook.

  • 3 of the top 10 IRCC pages are related to signing in.
  • Of the topics on the Immigration theme page, nearly half of the clicks are on My application.
  • Visitor visa is clearly the most popular Visit program receiving over 65% of the doormat clicks.
  • Express Entry remains the most popular immigration program, receiving close to 40% of the doormat clicks on the Immigrate topic page.
  • The top click on the revamped Work in Canada page is Work permits for foreign nationals.

Task success, ease and satisfaction scores remained stable.

Apply for an ETA continues to be the best-performing task, and Contact IRCC continues to be the lowest-performing task.

Contact us

Help Centre

The Contact IRCC page has the highest percentage of visits that click on the Help Centre link in the footer.

The top news release was about the 2025 Express Entry category-based draw.

The top statement was about the first-generation limit to Canadian citisenship by descent.

The top web notice was about family open work permits.

The top special measures content was about Ukraine.

The top campaigns were about Express Entry, eTAs and newcomer services.

More than half of visits are on mobile devices.
