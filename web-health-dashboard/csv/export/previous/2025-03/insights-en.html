Traffic remained relatively stable, with an increase in total visits and total pageviews.

Search engines (mainly Google) continue to drive most traffic to IRCC content, highlighting the importance of SEO (search engine optimization).

Of the traffic coming from social networks, over half of traffic is referred from Facebook.

  • Signing in remains a top task; 3 of the top 10 IRCC pages are related to sign-in.
  • Users want to manage their applications. Of the topics on the Immigration theme page, nearly half of the clicks are on My application.
  • Visitor visa is clearly the most popular Visit program receiving over 65% of the doormat clicks.
  • Express Entry remains the most popular immigration program, receiving close to 40% of the doormat clicks on the Immigrate topic page.
  • Close to half of doormat clicks on the Get a work permit page are for regular work permits for temporary workers.

Task success, ease and satisfaction scores remained stable.

Apply for an ETA continues to be the best-performing task, and Contact IRCC continues to be the lowest-performing task.
