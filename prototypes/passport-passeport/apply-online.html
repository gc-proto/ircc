Renew your passport online

We’re limiting the number of online applications

We’re currently limiting online applications to monitor system performance. You can start your application until we reach our daily limit. The limit resets at 7 a.m. and 7 p.m., Eastern Time.

You can still renew your passport in person or by mail.

Who can renew their passport online

The requirements to apply online are different from those for applying in person or by mail.

Make sure you meet all these requirements before creating an account:

  •  You’re applying to renew your own passport.
  •  Your home and mailing address is in Canada.
  •  Your current passport:
    • is a regular (blue) passport
    • was valid for 5 or 10 years
    • shows your place of birth
    • was issued when you were 16 years of age or older
    • was issued in the last 15 years and
      • is expired or
      • will expire in the next 6 months
  •  You’ll use the same name, date of birth, place of birth and gender identifier on your new passport.
  •  You don’t have any observations in your current passport.
    What is an observation?

    Observations are notes some people have on a separate page of their passport to explain special circumstances (like a name that was too long to be printed in full or if you have a diplomatic passport). Most people don’t have observations in their passport.

  •  You didn’t declare your current passport seized or surrendered.
  •  You don’t need your new passport for 20 business days (plus mailing time).

Your passport will be mailed to you if you apply online

It can take up to 20 business days to process your application, plus mailing time.

Apply in person if you need your passport sooner.

You need a digital photo to renew online

Your digital passport photo must be:

  • taken in person by a commercial photographer
  • be no more than 6 months old when you apply
  • must not be a scanned copy of a printed photo

It’s a good idea to get both a digital and printed photo. This will give you the flexibility to apply online, or by mail or in person.
