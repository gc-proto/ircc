Submit your application

There may be a service disruption at Canada Post

Due to the potential Canada Post labour disruption, you should use another courier if you want to apply by mail, or apply at a Service Canada Centre or passport office.

In preparation for a possible labour disruption, we’ll deliver your passport through another courier. Some delays may occur.

How you submit your application and what fees you pay depend on when you need the passport.

  • Your passport will be mailed to you unless you pay the extra fee for pickup service.
  • Some in-person locations can process your application quicker than others, and some offer express or urgent service for even faster processing.

Ways to renew your passport

In person

Regular processing is up to 20 business days.

Faster processing is available: Additional fees apply

  • Express processing (within 10 days)
  • Urgent processing (within 2 days)
  • Urgent pickup (next business day)

By mail

Processing time: up to 20 business days (including mailing time).

Online

Available for a limited number of people
  • Apply online with a digital photo
  • up tp 20 business days (including mailing time).
