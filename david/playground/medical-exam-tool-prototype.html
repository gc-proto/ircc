Find out if you need a medical exam

An immigration medical exam (IME) makes sure you’re allowed to enter Canada. You may need one if you want to come to Canada.

Answer a few questions to find out if:

  • you need a medical exam
  • you should get one before you apply or if you’ll be asked to get one later in the application process
  • a medical exam you’ve already done is still valid
What are you applying for?
What program are you applying under?
Are you applying for a super visa (for parents and grandparents to stay in Canada for more than 6 months)?
Are you currently in Canada?
Will you be working with agriculture or agri-food?

Examples include:

  • farming
  • butchering, meat, or poultry preparation
  • food science or preparation
  • planting and harvesting crops
  • raising livestock (animals)
  • harvesting fish or shellfish
  • other similar work
Will you be studying or working in a field where public health must be protected?

Examples include working:

  • in the health or medical fields
  • in a clinical laboratory
  • in primary or secondary education
  • in child care
  • in elderly care
  • in personal care or housekeeping
  • with people with disabilities
  • in other similar jobs
How long do you plan to stay?
Some people need a medical exam if they spent more than 6 months (in a row) in certain areas.

Enter each country or territory you’ve spent time in (lived in or visited) over the past 12 months and how long you stayed there for.

Start with where you were 12 months ago and end with where you are now.

The system will calculate if you need an exam.

Examples of how to calculate your time spent
  • If:
    • The current date is January 1, 2026
    • You live in France
    • You visited Germany from April 1, 2025 to April 17, 2025
    • You visited Mexico from September 1, 2025 to October 1, 2025

    You would enter

    Country or territory From To
    France January 1, 2025 April 1, 2025
    Germany April 1, 2025 April 17, 2025
    France April 17, 2025 September 1, 2025
    Mexico September 1, 2025 October 1, 2025
    France October 1, 2025 January 1, 2026
