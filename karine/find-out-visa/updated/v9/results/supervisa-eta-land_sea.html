  1. Super visa letter to visit for more than 6 months

    We’ll issue a super visa letter to you if we approve your super visa application. This is because you don’t need a visa to travel to Canada. The letter is what you show to the border officer at the Canadian port of entry.

    Cost:
    CAN $100
    You can stay in Canada for:
    Up to 5 years per visit

    When you need it

    • At the Canadian border (port of entry)
    • Not needed for flights within Canada or to leave Canada.

    If you aren't eligible for a super visa

    You need a valid passport or travel document to travel to Canada for up to 6 months (if you arrive by car, train, bus or boat).
