You need:

  1. Valid visa or electronic authorization (eTA)
  2. Valid passport or travel document
  3. Valid work permit

  1. Visa or eTA to travel to Canada

    If your visa is still valid

    Use your valid visa to return to Canada

    Do you need to apply for one

    No, you should already have one. We issued a visa to you when we approved your study permit. You can use this visa until it expires.

    When you need it
    • To board your flight to Canada
    • Not needed for flights within Canada or to leave Canada
    If your valid visa is in an expired passport or travel document

    Your visa is still valid and you can use it to travel to Canada. To avoid delays while travelling, you should apply for a new visa with your new passport.

    Exception

    You don’t need a visa if your work permit is still valid and you return directly to Canada from a visit to the United States or St. Pierre and Miquelon.

    If your visa is expired

    You can apply for an electronic travel authorization (eTA) to return to Canada

    Cost:
    CAN $7
    Valid for:
    5 years or until your passport expires (whichever comes first)

    Some eTAs can take several days to process. You should apply for one before you book your flight.

    When you need it
    • To board your flight to Canada
    • Not needed for flights within Canada or to leave Canada
    If you travel to Canada by car, train bus or boat instead

    You will need a valid visitor visa Opens in a new tab, not an eTA.

    Apply for an eTA (if you need one) Opens in a new tab

  2. Your valid passport or travel document

    This must be the passport or travel document linked to your eTA (if you have an eTA).

    When you need it

    To board your flight and at the Canadian border (port of entry)

  3. Your valid work permit

    When you need it

    At the Canadian border (port of entry)

    If your work permit expires soon

    Extend your work permit Opens in a new tab before you leave Canada. We’ll issue an eTA to you at the same time, if you don’t already have one. You can use this eTA to travel to Canada by air.
