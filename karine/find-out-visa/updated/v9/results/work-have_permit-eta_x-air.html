You can use your valid visitor visa or get an electronic travel authorization to return to Canada

If your visitor visa is still valid

What you need:
Visitor visa
Do you need to apply for one:
No, you should already have one

We automatically issued a visitor visa to you when we approved your work permit. You can travel back to Canada with this visa until it expires.

When you need it:
To board your flight to Canada (you don’t need it for flights within Canada or to leave Canada)
When you return to Canada, you also need:
  • Your valid work permit
  • Your valid passport or travel document
If your valid visa is in an expired passport or travel document:
Your visa is still valid, so bring it with you
There is one exception to this rule:
You don’t need a valid visitor visa if your work permit is still valid and you return directly to Canada from a visit to the United States or St. Pierre and Miquelon.

If your visitor visa is no longer valid

What you need:
Electronic travel authorization (eTA)
We approve most eTAs by email within minutes, but some applications can take several days if you need to submit supporting documents.
Valid for:
5 years or until your passport expires
Cost:
CAN $7
When you need it:
To board your flight to Canada (you don’t need it for flights within Canada or to leave Canada)
When you return to Canada, you also need:
  • Your valid work permit
  • The valid passport or travel document linked to your eTA
If you travel to Canada by car, train, bus or boat instead:
