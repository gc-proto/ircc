You need:

  1. Super visa for parents and grandparents
  2. Valid passport or travel document
  3. The documents you use in your super visa application

  1. Super visa to travel to Canada and visit for more than 6 months

    Cost:
    CAN $100
    Valid for:
    • Multiple entries
    • Up to a maximum of 10 years
    You can stay in Canada for:
    Up to 5 years per visit

    When you need it

    • To board your flight and at the Canadian border (port of entry)
    • Not needed for flights within Canada or to leave Canada

    If you aren't eligible for a super visa

    You need a visitor visa to visit Canada for up to 6 months.
