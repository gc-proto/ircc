You may need a Canadian passport

Most dual Canadian citizens need a valid Canadian passport to travel to Canada.

This is because, as a Canadian citizen, you’re not eligible for a visitor visa or an electronic travel authorization (eTA).

There are two exceptions to this rule:

  1. American-Canadian dual citizens with a valid US passport don’t need a Canadian passport.
  2. Citizens of a country or territory that does not need a visitor visa (they’re visa-exempt) can use a valid passport with that citizenship to travel to Canada by car, train, bus or boat (including cruise ship).
