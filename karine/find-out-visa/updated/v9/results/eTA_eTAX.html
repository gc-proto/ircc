You can get an electronic travel authorization (eTA)

Make sure you give yourself time to apply for and get your electronic travel authorization (eTA). We approve most eTAs by email within minutes, but some applications can take several days if we ask you to submit supporting documents.

What you need: electronic travel authorization (eTA) or a valid Canadian visitor visa

Valid for:

  • Per visit: up to 6 months
  • In total: 5 years (or until your passport expires)

Cost: CAN $7

When you need it: to board your flight to Canada (you don’t need it for flights within Canada or to leave Canada)

You also need: your passport or travel document (valid for your stay in Canada)

If you already have a valid Canadian visitor visa: you don’t need an eTA and can travel with your visa until it expires

If you travel to Canada by car, train, bus or boat instead: you will need a visitor visa, not an eTA

Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
