What you need:
A valid passport or travel document

We’ll also give you an electronic travel authorization (eTA) if we approve your work permit application.

When you need it:
At the Canadian border
You also need:
  • The passport or travel document that you used to apply for your work permit (valid for your stay in Canada)
  • The letter of introduction that says your work permit application is approved
If you travel to Canada by air instead:
You will need the valid eTA we gave you
Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
