You need:

  1. Transit visa
  2. Valid passport or travel document

Transit visa to travel through Canada

Cost:
Free
Valid for:

1 or 2 entries, depending on your travel plans

When you need it

  • To board your flight to Canada and at the Canadian border (port of entry)
  • Not needed for flights within Canada or to leave Canada

Your passport or travel document

Must be valid for your travel through Canada

Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
