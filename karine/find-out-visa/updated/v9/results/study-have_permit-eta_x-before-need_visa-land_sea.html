You need a visitor visa to return to Canada

What you need:
Visitor visa
Valid for:
Up to 10 years
Cost:
CAN $100
When you need it:
At the Canadian border
When you return to Canada, you also need:
  • Your valid study permit
  • Your valid passport or travel document
If you travel to Canada by air instead:
You can use the electronic travel authorization (eTA) we issued to you when we approved your study permit, if it’s still valid.
Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
