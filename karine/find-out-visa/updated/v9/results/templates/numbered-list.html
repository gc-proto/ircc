You need:

  1. Super visa letter for parents and grandparents
  2. Electronic travel authorization (eTA)
  3. Your valid passport or travel document
  4. The documents you used in your super visa application

  1. Super visa letter to visit for more than 6 months

    Cost:
    CAN $100
    Valid for
    • A single entry or multiple entries
    • Up to a maximum of 10 years

    When you need it

    • At the Canadian border (port of entry)

    If you aren't eligible for a super visa

    You need an eTA to visit Canada for up to 6 months

    Get a super visaOpens in a new tab

  2. Electronic travel authorization (eTA) to travel to Canada

    Cost:
    CAN $7
    Valid for
    Up to 5 years or until your passport expires (whichever comes first)

    Some eTAs can take several days to process. You should apply for one before you book your flight to Canada

    When you need it

    • To board your flight to Canada
    • Not needed to board flights within Canada or to leave Canada

    Apply for a new eTA (if you need one) Opens in a new tab
