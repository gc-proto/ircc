You need a visitor visa

What you need:
Visitor visa
Valid for:
  • Per visit: up to 6 months
  • In total: up to 10 years
Cost:
CAN $100
When you need it:
To board a flight to Canada (you don't need it to board flights within Canada or to leave Canada)
You also need:
Your passport or travel document (valid for your stay in Canada)
If you travel to Canada by air instead:
You may be eligible for a different document. Change your answer to find out.
Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
