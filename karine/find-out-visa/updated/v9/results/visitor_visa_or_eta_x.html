  1. Visitor visa

Visitor visa to travel to Canada

Cost:
CAN $100
Valid for:
  • A single entry or multiple entries
  • Up to a maximum of 10 years
You can stay in Canada for:
Up to 6 months per visit

When you need it

  • To board your flight to Canada and at the Canadian border (port of entry)
  • Not needed to board flights within Canada or to leave Canada

If you travel to Canada by air instead

You may need a different document. Update your answer to find out.
