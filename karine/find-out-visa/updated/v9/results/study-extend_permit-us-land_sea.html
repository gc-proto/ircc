You need a passport or another valid identity document

You need one of these:
  • Valid passport
  • Birth certificate
  • Certificate of citizenship or naturalization
  • Certificate of Indian status (and photo identification)
When you need it:
At the Canadian border
When you return to Canada, you also need:
Your valid study permit
If your study permit expires soon:
Extend it before you leave Canada
