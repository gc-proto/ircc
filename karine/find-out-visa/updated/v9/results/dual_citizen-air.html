You need a valid Canadian passport to travel to Canada

As a dual Canadian citizen, you must have a valid Canadian passport to board your flight to Canada. You won’t be able to board your flight without one.

There is only one exception to this rule: American-Canadian dual citizens with a valid US passport don’t need a Canadian passport. If you’re an American-Canadian dual citizen you will still need to carry proper identification and meet the basic requirements to enter Canada Opens in a new tab.
