You need a valid Canadian passport to travel to Canada

What you need:
A valid Canadian passport
When you need it:
To board your flight to Canada (you won’t be able to board your flight without one)
There is only one exception to this rule:

American-Canadian dual citizens with valid US passports don’t need Canadian passports. They must still carry proper identification Opens in a new tab and meet the basic requirements to enter Canada Opens in a new tab.
