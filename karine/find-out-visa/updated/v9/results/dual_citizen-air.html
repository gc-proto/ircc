You need a valid Canadian passport to travel to Canada

What you need:
A valid Canadian passport
Why you need it:
As a Canadian citizen, you’re not eligible for a visitor visa or an electronic travel authorization (eTA)
When you need it:
To board your flight to Canada (you won’t be able to board your flight without one)
American-Canadian dual citizens:
You can also travel with your valid US passport, but we recommend travelling with both passports to make your travel easier. You should also always carry proper identification that shows your Canadian citizenship Opens in a new tab. You still need to meet the basic requirements to enter Canada Opens in a new tab.
