You need a valid electronic travel authorization to return to Canada

What you need:

Electronic travel authorization (eTA)

We approve most eTAs by email within minutes, but some applications can take several days if you need to submit supporting documents.
Do you need to apply for one:
Only if your eTA expired and you plan to leave and return to Canada
Cost:
CAN $7
When you need it:
To board a flight back to Canada (you don’t need it to board flights within Canada or to leave Canada)
When you return to Canada, you also need:
  • Your valid study permit
  • The passport or travel document linked to your eTA (valid for your stay in Canada)
If your study permit expires soon:

Extend it before you apply for a new eTA.

We’ll issue you a new eTA at the same time.
