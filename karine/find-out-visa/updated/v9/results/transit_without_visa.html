You need a transit visa

What you need: a transit visa

Valid for: 1 or 2 entries, depending on your travel plans

Cost: Free

When you need it: to connect between your international flights in Canada

You also need: your passport or travel document (valid for your travel through Canada)

When you need a visitor visa instead of a transit visa:

  • your international flights are more than 48 hours apart
  • you decide to visit Canada while you’re here
  • you travel to Canada by car, train, bus or boat instead

If you transit (travel) through Canada to or from the United States: you may be able to transit without a visa if you meet the conditions for it
