You need a transit visa

What you need:
A transit visa
Valid for:
1 or 2 entries, depending on your travel plans
Cost:
Free
When you need it:
To board a flight to Canada (you don't need it to board flights within Canada or to leave Canada)
You also need:
Your passport or travel document (valid for your travel through Canada)
When you need a visitor visa instead of a transit visa:
  • Your international flights are more than 48 hours apart
  • You decide to visit Canada while you’re here
  • You travel to Canada by car, train, bus or boat instead

Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
