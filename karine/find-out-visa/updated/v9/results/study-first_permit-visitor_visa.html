We’ll give you a visitor visa if we approve your study permit application

What you need:
Visitor visa
Do you need to apply for one:
No, it’s included with your study permit application
Cost:
Included with your study permit application fees
When you need it:
To board a flight to Canada (you don’t need it to board flights within Canada or to leave Canada)
You also need:
  • The passport or travel document with your visa in it (valid for your stay in Canada)
  • The letter of introduction that says your study permit application is approved
  • A copy of your letter of acceptance for your school
Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
