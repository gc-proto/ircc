You can get an electronic travel authorization (eTA)

What you need:
Electronic travel authorization (eTA)

We approve most eTAs by email within minutes, but some applications can take several days if you need to submit supporting documents.

Valid for:
  • Per visit: up to 6 months
  • In total: 5 years (or until your passport expires)
Cost:
CAN $7
When you need it:
To board your flight to Canada (you don’t need it for flights within Canada or to leave Canada)
You also need:
Your passport or travel document (valid for your stay in Canada)

  • security
  • medical conditions
