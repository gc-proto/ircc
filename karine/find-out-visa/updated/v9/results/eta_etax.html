Electronic travel authorization (eTA) to travel to Canada

Cost:
CAN $7
Valid for:

5 years or until your passport expires (whichever comes first)

You can stay in Canada for:
Up to 6 months per visit

Some eTA applications can take several days to process. You should apply for one before you book your flight to Canada.

When you need it

  • To board your flight to Canada
  • Not needed for flights within Canada or to leave Canada

Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
