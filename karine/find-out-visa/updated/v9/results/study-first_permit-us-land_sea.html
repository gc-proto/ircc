  1. Valid passport or travel document

  3. A copy of your letter of acceptance for your school

  1. Your valid passport or travel document

    This must be the passport or travel document you used to apply for your study permit.

    When you need it

    At the Canadian border (port of entry)

  2. Your letter of introduction

    This is the letter we send you that says your study permit application is approved.

    When you need it

    At the Canadian border (port of entry)

  3. Your letter of acceptance from your school

    When you need it

    At the Canadian border (port of entry)
