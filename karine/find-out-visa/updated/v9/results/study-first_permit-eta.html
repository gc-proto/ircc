You need:

  1. Electronic travel authorization (eTA) (issued if we approve your study permit)
  2. Valid passport or travel document
  3. Study permit approval (letter of introduction)

  1. Electronic travel authorization to travel to Canada

    Cost:
    Included with your study permit application fees

    We'll issue an eTA to you if we approve your study permit application

    You don’t need to apply for an eTA separately. We’ll issue an eTA to you if we approve your study permit application.

    When you need it

    • To board your flight to Canada
    • Not needed for flights within Canada or to leave Canada

    Apply for a study permitOpens in a new tab

  2. Your valid passport or travel document

    This must be the passport or travel document linked to your eTA.

    When you need it

    To board your flight to Canada and at the Canadian border (port of entry)

  3. Study permit approval (letter of introduction)

    If we approve your study permit application, we'll issue a letter of introduction to you. Bring this letter with you when you travel to Canada. A border officer will need it to issue your study permit.

    When you need it

    At the Canadian border (port of entry)

Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
