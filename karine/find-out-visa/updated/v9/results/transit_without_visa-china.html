You need a transit visa

What you need:
A transit visa
Valid for:
1 or 2 entries, depending on your travel plans
Cost:
Free
When you need it:
To connect between your international flights in Canada
You also need:
Your passport or travel document (valid for your travel through Canada)
When you need a visitor visa instead of a transit visa:
  • Your international flights are more than 48 hours apart
  • You decide to visit Canada while you’re here
  • You travel to Canada by car, train, bus or boat instead
If you transit (travel) through Canada to or from the United States:
You may be able to transit without a visa if you meet the conditions for it
