You need:

  1. Super visa letter for parents and grandparents
  2. Electronic travel authorization (eTA)
  3. Your valid passport or travel document
  4. The documents you used in your super visa application

  1. Super visa letter to visit for more than 6 months

    We’ll issue a super visa letter to you if we approve your super visa application. This is because you don’t need a visa to travel to Canada. The letter is what you show to the border officer at the Canadian port of entry.

    Cost
    CAN $100
    You can stay in Canada for
    Up to 5 years per visit

    When you need it

    At the Canadian border (port of entry)

    If you aren't eligible for a super visa

    You need an eTA to visit Canada for up to 6 months

    Apply for a super visaOpens in a new tab

  2. Electronic travel authorization (eTA) to travel to Canada

    Cost
    CAN $7
    Valid for
    Up to 5 years or until your passport expires (whichever comes first)

    When you need it

    • To board your flight to Canada
    • Not needed to board flights within Canada or to leave Canada

    Apply for a new eTA (if you need one) Opens in a new tab

    When you need it

    To board your flight and at the Canadian border (port of entry)

Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
