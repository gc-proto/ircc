You need your Canadian passport, permanent resident card or another identification document 

Canadian citizens 

You need one of these:
  • A valid Canadian passport Opens in a new tab
  • An identification document that supports your identity

    (read the list of identification documents the CBSA accepts)

When you need it:
At the Canadian border (port of entry)
Do you need anything else:
No, as a Canadian citizen, you don’t need a visa or permit to enter, study or work in Canada

Permanent residents of Canada 

You need both of these:
  • A valid passport or travel document
  • A valid permanent resident (PR) card
When you need it:
At the Canadian border (port of entry)
Do you need anything else:
No, as a permanent resident of Canada, you don’t need a visa or permit to enter, study or work in Canada
If you're travelling by private vehicle (one you own, rent or borrow):
There may be other documents you can use at the port of entry. Read the list of identification documents the CBSA accepts.
If you don't have a PR card and you’re outside of Canada

You must apply for a permanent resident travel document (PRTD). You will need this document to travel to Canada by air. You can apply for a new PR card once you’re back in Canada.
