You need an electronic travel authorization (eTA)

What you need:

Electronic travel authorization (eTA)

We approve most eTAs by email within minutes, but some applications can take several days if you need to submit supporting documents. You should apply for an eTA before you book your flight to Canada.

Valid for:

5 years or until your passport expires (whichever comes first)

Most visitors can stay for up to 6 months per visit. A border services officer at the port of entry will decide how long you can stay in Canada.

Cost:
CAN $7
When you need it:
To board your flight to Canada (you don’t need it for flights within Canada or to leave Canada)
You also need:
Your passport or travel document (valid for your stay in Canada)
Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
