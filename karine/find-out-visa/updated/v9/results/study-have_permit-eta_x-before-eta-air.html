You can use your valid electronic travel authorization to return to Canada

What you need:
Electronic travel authorization (eTA)
Do you need to apply for one:
No, you should already have one

We automatically issued an eTA to you when we approved your study permit. You can travel back to Canada with it until it, or your passport, expires.

When you need it:
To board a flight back to Canada (you don’t need it to board flights within Canada or to leave Canada)
When you return to Canada, you also need:
  • Your valid study permit
  • The valid passport or travel document linked to your eTA
If your eTA expired:
Apply for a new eTA
If you got a new passport or travel document:
You need to apply for a new eTA (the eTA attached to your old document will no longer be valid)
If you travel to Canada by car, train, bus or boat instead:
You will need a valid visitor visa , not an eTA
