You need a valid visitor visa to return to Canada

What you need:
Visitor visa
Do you need to apply for one:
Only if your visa expired and you plan to leave and return to Canada
Cost:
CAN $100
When you need it:
To board a flight back to Canada (you don’t need it to board flights within Canada or to leave Canada)
When you return to Canada, you also need:
  • Your valid work permit
  • Your valid passport or travel document
If your work permit expires soon:
Extend it before you apply for a new visitor visa or leave Canada
If your valid visa is in an expired passport or travel document:
Your visa is still valid, so bring it with you
If your visa expired or was only valid for a single entry:
Apply for a new visitor visa
There is one exception to this rule:
You don’t need a valid visitor visa if your work permit is still valid and you return directly to Canada from a visit to the United States or St. Pierre and Miquelon.
