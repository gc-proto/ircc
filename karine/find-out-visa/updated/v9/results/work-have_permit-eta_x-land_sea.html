  1. Visitor visa to travel to Canada

    Do you need to apply for one:

    No, you should already have one. We issued a visa to you when we approved your work permit. You can use this visa until it expires.

    When you need it

    At the Canadian border (port of entry)

    If your valid visa is in an expired passport or travel document

    Your visa is still valid and you can use it to travel to Canada. To avoid delays while travelling, you should apply for a new visa with your new, valid passport.

    If your visa expired or was only valid for a single entry

    Apply for a new visa

    If you travel to Canada by air instead

    You may need a different document. Update your answer to find out.
