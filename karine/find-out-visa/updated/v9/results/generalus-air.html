You need a valid passport to travel to Canada by air

What you need:
Passport (valid for your stay in Canada)
When you need it:
To board your flight to Canada
You can also use:
A valid NEXUS membership card
Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
