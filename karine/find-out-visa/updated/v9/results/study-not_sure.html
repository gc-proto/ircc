You need to find out if you need a study permit

You may or may not need a permit to study in Canada, depending on your situation.

You need to:

  • Go to our study permit tool to find out if you need a permit.
  • Come back to confirm what you need to travel to Canada.
Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
