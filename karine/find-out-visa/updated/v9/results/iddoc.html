You need a valid passport or travel document

What you need: a passport or travel document (valid for your stay in Canada)

When you need it: at the Canadian border

If you travel to Canada by air instead: you will need an electronic travel authorization (eTA) as well

Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
