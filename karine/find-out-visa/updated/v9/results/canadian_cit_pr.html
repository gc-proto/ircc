You need your Canadian passport, permanent resident card or another identification document

Canadian citizens

Travelling by air:

You must have a valid Canadian passport.

As a Canadian citizen, you don’t need a visa or permit to enter, study or work in Canada.

Travelling by car, train, bus or boat (including cruise ships):

You need one of these:

  • a valid Canadian passport
  • an identification document that supports your identity or citizenship

As a Canadian citizen, you don’t need a visa or permit to enter, study or work in Canada.

Permanent residents of Canada

Travelling by commercial vehicle (like a plane, train, bus or boat):

You must have a permanent resident (PR) card to travel to Canada.

As a permanent resident of Canada, you don’t need a visa or permit to enter, study or work in Canada.

Permanent resident cards Opens in a new tab

If you don’t have a PR card and you’re outside of Canada

You must apply for a permanent resident travel document (PRTD). You will need this document to travel to Canada by air. Make sure to apply for a new PR card once you’re back in Canada.
