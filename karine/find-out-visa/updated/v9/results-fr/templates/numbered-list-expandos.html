Vous avez besoin de ce qui suit :

  1. A valid visa or an electronic authorization (eTA)
  2. Your valid passport or travel document
  3. Your valid study permit

  1. Visa or eTA to travel to Canada

    Your visa is still valid

    Use your valid visa to return to Canada

    Do you need to apply for one

    No, you should already have one. We issued a visa to you when we approved your study permit. You can use this visa until it expires.

    When you need it
    • Pour monter à bord de votre vol
    • Non requis pour les vols à l’intérieur du Canada ou pour quitter le Canada
    If your valid visa is in an expired passport or travel document

    Your visa is still valid and you can use it to travel to Canada. To avoid delays while travelling, you should apply for a new visa with your new passport.

    Exception

    You don’t need a visa if your study permit is still valid and you return directly to Canada from a visit to the United States or St. Pierre and Miquelon.

    Your visa is expired
    Coût :
    CAN $7
    Valid for
    Up to 5 years or until your passport expires (whichever comes first)

    You can get an electronic travel authorization (eTA) to return to Canada

    Some eTA applications can take several days to process. You should apply for one before you book your flight.

    When you need it
    • Pour monter à bord de votre vol
    • Non requis pour les vols à l’intérieur du Canada ou pour quitter le Canada
    If you travel to Canada by car, train bus or boat instead

    You will need a valid visitor visa, not an eTA.
