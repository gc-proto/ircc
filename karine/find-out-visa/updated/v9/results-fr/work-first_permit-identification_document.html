Vous avez besoin de ce qui suit :

  1. Valid passport or travel document
  2. Work permit approval (letter of introduction)

  1. Your valid passport or travel document

    This must be the passport or travel document you use to apply for your work permit.

    We’ll issue an electronic travel authorization (eTA) to you if we approve your work permit application, but you’ll only need it if you travel to Canada by air.

    Quand en avez-vous besoin?

    À la frontière canadienne (point d’entrée)

    If you travel to Canada by air instead

    You will need the eTA we issue to you.

  2. Work permit approval (letter of introduction)

    If we approve your work permit application, we'll issue a letter of introduction to you. Bring this letter with you when you travel to Canada. A border officer will need it to issue your work permit.

    Quand en avez-vous besoin?

    À la frontière canadienne (point d’entrée)
