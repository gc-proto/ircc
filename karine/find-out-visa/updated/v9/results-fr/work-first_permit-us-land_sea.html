  1. Your valid passport or travel document

    This must be the passport or travel document you used to apply for your work permit.

    Quand en avez-vous besoin?

  2. Work permit approval (letter of introduction)

    If we approve your work permit application, we'll issue a letter of introduction to you. Bring this letter with you when you travel to Canada. A border officer will need it to issue your work permit.
