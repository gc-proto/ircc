Vous avez besoin de ce qui suit :

  1. Electronic travel authorization (eTA)
  2. Valid passport or travel document
  3. Valid study permit

  1. Electronic travel authorization to travel to Canada

    You should already have a valid eTA

    We issued an eTA to you when we approved your study permit. You can use it until it expires.

    Quand en avez-vous besoin

    • To board your flight to Canada
    • Not needed for flights within Canada or to leave Canada

    If your eTA expired

    Apply for a new eTA.

    Apply for a new eTA (if you need one)Ouvre dans un nouvel onglet

  2. Your valid passport or travel document

    This must be the passport or travel document linked to your eTA.

    Quand en avez-vous besoin

    To board your flight to Canada and at the Canadian border (port of entry)

    If you have a new passport or travel document

    You need to apply for a new eTA. The eTA linked to your old document will no longer be valid.

  3. Your valid study permit

    Quand en avez-vous besoin

    At the Canadian border (port of entry)

    If your study permit expires soon

    Extend your study permitOuvre dans un nouvel onglet before you apply for a new eTA or leave Canada. We'll issue a new eTA to you at the same time.
