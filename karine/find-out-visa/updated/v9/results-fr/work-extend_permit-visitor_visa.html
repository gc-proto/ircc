Vous avez besoin de ce qui suit :

  1. Visitor visa
  2. Valid passport or travel document
  3. Valid work permit

  1. Visitor visa to return to Canada

    Cost:
    CAN $100

    You may still have a valid visa

    You only need to apply for a new visa if your existing visa has expired and you plan to leave and return to Canada.

    Quand en avez-vous besoin?

    • To board your flight to Canada and at the Canadian border (port of entry)
    • Not needed for flights within Canada or to leave Canada

    If your valid visa is in an expired passport or travel document

    Your visa is still valid and you can use it to travel to Canada. To avoid delays while travelling, you should apply for a new visa with your new, valid passport.

    If your visa expired or was only valid for a single entry

    Apply for a new visa.

    Exception

    You don’t need a valid visa if your work permit is still valid and you return directly to Canada from a visit to the United States or St. Pierre and Miquelon.

    Apply for a new visitor visa (if you need one) Ouvre dans un nouvel onglet

  2. Your valid passport or travel document

    Quand en avez-vous besoin?

    To board your flight and at the Canadian border (port of entry)

  3. Your valid work permit

    Quand en avez-vous besoin?

    À la frontière canadienne (point d’entrée)

    If your work permit expires soon

    Extend your work permit Ouvre dans un nouvel onglet before you apply for a new visa or leave Canada.
