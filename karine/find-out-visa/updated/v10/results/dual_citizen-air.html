    Passports are the only universally accepted identification document when you’re travelling. A Canadian passport proves you have a right to return to Canada.

    Why you need it

    As a dual Canadian citizen, you’re not eligible for a visitor visa or an electronic travel authorization (eTA).

    When you need it

    To board your flight to Canada

    American-Canadian dual citizens

    You can travel with your valid US passport, but we recommend travelling with both passports to make your travel easier.
