You must have a valid passport or travel document

A passport is the only universally accepted identification document when you’re travelling.

Depending on what you’re planning to do in Canada, you may also need:

  • a visitor visa, super visa or transit visa
  • an electronic travel authorization (eTA)
  • a work permit or study permit

Once you have a passport, use this tool again to find out what you need.
