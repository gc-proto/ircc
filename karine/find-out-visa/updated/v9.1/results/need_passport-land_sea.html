You must have a valid passport or travel document

A passport is the only universally accepted identification document when you’re travelling.

Depending on what you’re planning to do in Canada, you may also need:

  • a visitor visa or super visa
  • a work permit or study permit
  • an electronic travel authorization (eTA), if you travel by air instead

Once you have a passport, use this tool again to find out what you need.

US citizens don’t need a passport to enter Canada by land or sea

US citizens don’t need a passport to enter Canada by car, train, bus or boat (including cruise ship). You can use one of these identification documents instead:

  • birth certificate
  • certificate of citizenship or naturalization
  • certificate of Indian status (with photo identification)
Reasons you might not be allowed in Canada (including past convictions)

Some people aren't allowed to come to Canada. You could be inadmissible for reasons like:

  • convictions (including driving while impaired)
  • security
  • medical conditions
